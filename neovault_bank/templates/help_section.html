You can open a new account in several ways:

  • Online: Click on "Open Account" in your dashboard
  • Mobile App: Use our mobile banking app
  • Branch: Visit any of our 500+ branches nationwide

Documents required:

  • National ID Card/Passport
  • Recent photograph
  • Proof of address
  • Tax Identification Number (if applicable)

If you've forgotten your password:

  1. Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page
  2. Enter your registered email address
  3. Check your email for a password reset link
  4. Click the link and create a new strong password
  5. Log in with your new password
If you don't receive the email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder or contact our support team.

If your card is lost or stolen, take immediate action:

  1. Block your card immediately:
    • Through internet banking
    • Via our mobile app
    • Call our 24/7 helpline: 1-800-NEOVAULT
  2. Report the incident: Contact our fraud department
  3. Monitor your account: Check for unauthorized transactions
  4. Request a replacement card: Free replacement within 3-5 business days
You are not liable for unauthorized transactions if reported within 24 hours.

To enable two-factor authentication (2FA):

  1. Go to your Profile Settings
  2. Click on "Security Settings"
  3. Select "Enable Two-Factor Authentication"
  4. Choose your preferred method:
    • SMS: Receive codes via text message
    • Authenticator App: Use Google Authenticator or similar
    • Email: Receive codes via email
  5. Follow the setup instructions
  6. Test the authentication process

Recommended: Use an authenticator app for the highest level of security.
